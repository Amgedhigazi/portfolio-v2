About me

The story behind
the work

Born in Omdurman, Sudan, I received my early training at the prestigious College of Fine and Applied Arts in Khartoum, and upon graduation apprenticed for the legendary Prof. Hassan Alhadi.

I have worked for a diverse group of advertising, design, and editorial clients — including The Apex Press & Publishing in Muscat, Sultanate of Oman, where I served as Art Director and journalist photographer specialising in cultural, beauty, and fashion photography.

Through my years of practical experience and special knowledge in creative design and marketing, I can support you and your company in the best possible way to pursue your rightful place ahead of the competition.

Currently working with the African Tide Association in Germany. I have had many solo and group exhibitions internationally.
