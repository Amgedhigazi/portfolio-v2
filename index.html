After a successful year-long training programme, I now focus on technologies like ReactJS, JavaScript, HTML5, CSS3, Responsive Web Design, and NodeJS — delivering high-quality solutions as a reliable team player.

Beyond my technical skills, I am a versatile creative talent with experience in visual art, photography, and graphic design. The combination of art and software development gives my work a unique perspective — my passion lies in using technology to promote creativity and visual expression.

Born in Omdurman, Sudan. Trained at the College of Fine & Applied Arts, Khartoum. Worked across Sudan, India, Oman, and now Germany.
